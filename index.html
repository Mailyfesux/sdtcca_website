Winter Quarter 2014

Welcome back!

Hope you all had an enjoyable Winter break. We're sure you all have a lot to share at the Winter Orientation which will be on the 24th of this month. The orientation will be held at "TBA". If you can't find it, give us a call and we'll be sure to guide you to us.

Again, if you haven't been able to attend any of our events yet, don't worry! New members are always welcome to join in any of our upcoming events. For most events, the meeting place will be on the UCSD campus at Peterson Loop (the side facing the faculty club parking lot). Check the Google calendar, our mailing list updates, or the Facebook group for exact times and dates of upcoming events.

We will sometimes ask you to bring your "EPK" to events. In otherwords, it's just tupperware; but, it actually stands for Environmental Protection Kit! Read about it here: About Us.

Please note that while officers are required to wear uniforms to most official Tzu Ching events, that doesn't mean that other members cannot also wear uniforms as well! If you've logged over 20 hours with Tzu Ching, you are eligible to get your own uniform. Contact us for more information.

Study Hard!

Your Tzu Ching Officers


The Tzu Chi Collegiate Association (Tzu Ching) is a college organization that carries out the missions of the international Tzu Chi Foundation--charity, education, culture, and medicine. Tzu Ching provides an opportunity for volunteer work within the local area that is rewarding, meaningful, and manageable during one's busy college life.

By providing and caring for our school and community, we seek to cultivate ourselves to become better individuals. After all, "Tzu Ching" means "compassionate youth", and we believe that anyone can bring out the kindness and compassion in their heart. Students of any race and religion are welcome to join our Tzu Ching family here at UCSD! :)
